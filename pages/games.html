Pokemon

These are my favourite Pokemon games.


FireRed

The Kanto region is where it all started and it's just a grand adventure.

SoulSilver

The Johto region has a unique flair and even includes a trip back to Kanto.

Alpha Sapphire

Hoenn is a classic, and the Delta Episode was just superb.

Platinum

Sinnoh is my home, and the Distortion World was awesome. So nostalgic.



Black

Unova has really grown on me and I can't wait to go back all over again.

X

The Kalos region and its graphics blew my mind and stoked an old fire once upon a time.

Moon

Playing through the Alola region in the night was a nice vacation.

Sword

The Galar region was a quaint stroll with its own charms.
